Reasons I love skiing

I fell in love with skiing about three years ago when I first gave it a try. I still consider myself a newbie, I have only used Green and Blue slopes, but I hope to be able to go down a Diamond slope one day. My ultimate goal would be to ride alongside my ski mates on a Double Diamond slope. I guess we'll see about that. Why I love this sport one might ask. For many reasons, but just to name a few:

  • it is really peaceful and meditative;
  • you constantly have to look for a balance, more like a harmony between your skis, the snow and the sopes;
  • it offers access to truly amazing scenery; floating through the landscape is an incrdible feeling;

Slopes colors

Maybe you are wondering what the colors I mentioned before represent. They indicate the difficulty level of the slopes. The table below will help you understand them.

Color Difficulty Symbol Slope gradient
Green Easy ⚫ 25% or less
Blue Intermediate ☐ 25% - 40%
Black Difficult ♦ 40% or more
Expert ♦ ♦ 40% or more with obstacles

Caution

Although skiing is an awesome activity, it can also be dangerous. If you want to test it out, make sure you sign up for a class first so you are shown a few basic moves and postures. And remember to always wear a helmet. Even this penguin here knows that.
